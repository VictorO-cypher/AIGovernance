# Project 05: High-Risk AI Documentation & Conformity Pack

## Scenario

**Organisation:** Federal Ministry of Health (FMoH) / Nigeria Centre for Disease Control (NCDC)  
**System:** NCDC Integrated Disease Surveillance Platform — AI Module (FMoH-AI-001)  
**Context:** Following the establishment of FMoH's AI Governance Programme and the lessons learned from the MamaAlert incident (Project 04), the AI Review Committee has directed that all High Risk AI systems in the FMoH portfolio must compile a formal documentation pack. The NCDC Disease Surveillance Platform is the first system to undergo this process.

This project compiles the full governance documentation pack for the NCDC AI-powered outbreak prediction module — covering intended purpose, risk management, data governance, human oversight, logging, and performance monitoring.

---

## What This Project Demonstrates

- Detailed knowledge of EU AI Act Articles 9–17 obligations for high-risk AI systems (applied as international benchmark)
- Understanding of what comprehensive technical and governance documentation looks like in practice
- Ability to structure compliance documentation in a defensible, auditable format
- Practical knowledge of what regulators, auditors, and international donors look for in AI conformity documentation
- Understanding that documentation is not bureaucracy — it is the evidence that governance is real
- Applied knowledge of WHO AI Ethics guidance and NIST AI RMF in a critical public health infrastructure context

## A Note on Scope

The EU AI Act places primary documentation obligations on AI providers — those who develop or place AI systems on the market. FMoH/NCDC is primarily a **deployer** of the AI module, which was developed with technical support from Africa CDC and WHO AFRO.

However, deployers of high-risk AI systems have significant obligations, including:

- Ensuring the system is used in accordance with its intended purpose
- Ensuring required human oversight is in place
- Monitoring system performance in the deployed context
- Reporting serious incidents

This documentation pack covers both the **provider documentation** that FMoH must obtain from Africa CDC/WHO AFRO, and the **deployer documentation** that FMoH/NCDC must produce itself. Both are clearly labelled throughout.
