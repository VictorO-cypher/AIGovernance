## How to Score

Assign a maturity level of **1–5** for each domain based on evidence gathered.

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| 1 — Initial | Absent or entirely ad hoc |
| 2 — Developing | Some practices exist but inconsistently applied |
| 3 — Defined | Documented and consistently applied |
| 4 — Managed | Data-driven, actively monitored and reported |
| 5 — Optimised | Continuous improvement with sector contribution |
